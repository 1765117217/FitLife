Workout Nutrition

Whey Protein

Protein/Recovery

Fast digesting; ideal within 30–60 min post-workout to support recovery.

Mass Gainer

Protein/Recovery Energy/Carbs

High-calorie formula combining protein and carbs; useful for bulking and weight gain.

Energy Gel

Energy/Carbs

Staple for endurance fueling; scheduled intake helps prevent “hitting the wall”.

Electrolyte Drink

Electrolytes/Hydration

Replenish Na/K/Mg; reduce cramping and dehydration risk—vital in hot conditions.

BCAA

Creatine Monohydrate

Tips (sample copy you can use in the report)

  • 30–60 min pre-workout carbs (e.g., banana/energy bar) can improve performance.
  • For endurance 60–90 min, add carbs + electrolytes as needed (gels + electrolyte drink).
  • Within 30–60 min post-workout, prioritize quality protein (e.g., whey) plus some carbs for recovery.
  • Diet first; supplements are supplemental. Balanced nutrition and sleep matter more than pills/powders.
